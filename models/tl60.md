# TL60 RGB / RGB-2 / RGB-3

A 60 cm RGB tube in Neewer's Infinity tube line. Three generations exist: the base **TL60 RGB**
(light-type 32) and the **TL60 RGB-2** (type 59) and **TL60 RGB-3** (type 115). The `-2` and
`-3` are the fixtures that carry the **Streamer** feature; the base TL60 RGB does not.

| | Base | RGB-2 | RGB-3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Light-type | 32 | 59 | 115 |
| Advertised serial | `NW-20210036` | `NW-20230064` | `NW-20240061` |
| Latest firmware | 1.3.10 | 2.4.8 | 3.0.5 |
| CCT range | 2500–10000 K | 2500–10000 K | 2500–10000 K |
| Battery¹ | none¹ | 14.4 V / 3000 mAh¹ | 14.4 V / 3000 mAh¹ |
| Streamer / FX-Flow | No | Yes | Yes |
| Wired DMX512 port | Yes | Yes | Yes |

¹ A TL60 unit is confirmed at a built-in (non-removable) **14.4 V / 3000 mAh** pack (44.4 Wh per
the product listing). Neewer's configuration marks the base TL60 RGB (type 32) with no battery;
whether that variant is genuinely mains-only is unverified.

**Charger:** Neewer **NT-200240D2**, 20 V / 2.4 A / 48 W
([label](../assets/neewer-psu-nt-200240d2-20v-2.4a-48w.jpg)) — a third distinct 20 V brick,
below the TL90C's 72 W and the TL120C's 100 W. All three tube chargers are 20 V barrel PSUs;
only the amperage scales with the light.

## Capabilities

- **HSI, CCT (with GM), RGBCW** — full colour.
- **No CIE xy.** Unlike the TL90C/TL120C, the TL60 family does not advertise the xy
  coordinate mode.
- **Pixel effects** (`pixelEffectClassify` 1) — vendor-listed but **unverified** on the TL60
  (same status as the TL90C; confirmed only on the TL120C).
- **Streamer / FX-Flow** on the RGB-2 and RGB-3 only.

## Streamer

The TL60 RGB-2 and RGB-3 are the only models in the entire catalogue that support the
**Streamer** subsystem (what the vendor UI labels "FX Flow"). A fixture self-reports it: a
`0xC4` streamer-support query returns a `0x17` reply with value `01`. The `0xC0` realtime play
and `0xBF` multi-fixture download frames are documented in
[../pixel-and-streamer.md](../pixel-and-streamer.md).

Streamer is a group feature — it renders across two or more fixtures. On a single tube the
app streams per-MAC `0x8F`/`0x90` colour frames instead of using Streamer.

## Protocol notes

- **Extended colour is by-MAC only**, as with the rest of the TL tube line.
- The firmware-version ASCII tail follows the generation: 2.x reports `RGB-2`, 3.x reports
  `RGB-3`. This tracks firmware, not the physical model — the advertised serial is the
  identifier. Both firmware 3.0.3 and 3.0.5 are TL60 RGB-3.

## Firmware

Delivered by the custom `0x78` block OTA protocol (8 colour blocks per fixture in Streamer
downloads). See [../firmware.md](../firmware.md).
